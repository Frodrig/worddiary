Word diary is a beautiful and minimalist diary where you can write a unique word to describe your day.


How was your day today? Special? Sad? Unforgettable? Full of memories? Nothing more stylish than to describe your day with just one word. A word that will take you back in time when you read it again.


This app is now available with four beautiful calligraphic fonts and different soft colours to customize your diary. You can use the calendar to flip through the diary and write the word that inspired your day.
